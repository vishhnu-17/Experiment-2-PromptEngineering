# EX-02-Cross-Platform-Prompting-Evaluating-Diverse-Techniques-in-AI-Powered-Content-Writing

## AIM

To evaluate and compare the effectiveness of prompting techniques (zero-shot, few-shot, chain-of-thought, role-based) across different AI platforms (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Copilot) in a specific task: content writing.

---

## Scenario:

You are part of a digital marketing team for an eco-friendly startup launching a reusable water bottle brand. Your task is to generate engaging promotional content for social media campaigns using multiple AI platforms and prompting strategies.

Your goal is to determine which combination of prompting technique + platform provides the best output in terms of:

* Creativity
* Clarity
* Engagement
* Speed
* User experience

---

## Prompting Techniques Used

### 1. Zero-Shot Prompting

"Write a short promotional post for an eco-friendly reusable water bottle."

### 2. Few-Shot Prompting

"Here are 2 examples of engaging eco-product advertisements. Generate a similar promotional post for a reusable water bottle."

### 3. Chain-of-Thought Prompting

"Think step-by-step about the target audience, product benefits, and emotional appeal, then create the final promotional content."

### 4. Role-Based Prompting

"Act as a professional digital marketer and create a social media advertisement for a reusable water bottle brand."

---

### Final Conclusion

Applying different prompting techniques across multiple AI platforms shows that output quality in content writing depends greatly on both the prompting strategy and the AI model used. Role-based prompting consistently generated the most engaging and professional promotional content, while zero-shot prompting produced the fastest responses. Few-shot prompting improved structure and tone consistency, whereas chain-of-thought prompting enhanced creativity and audience-focused messaging.

Thus, selecting the appropriate prompting method along with the right AI platform is essential for generating high-quality marketing and promotional content.
